Our Story

Special Software (India) Pvt. Ltd. (SSI) is a one-stop solution for all your business and personal IT needs.


We offer turnkey software solutions and business automation services, to industries and individuals seeking technological transformation. Our expertise lies in developing bespoke software applications that address critical business challenges, ensuring a seamless blend of functionality, scalability, and innovation.


We also supply consumer electronics such as refrigerators, TVs, laptops, washing machines, and specialized hardware like industrial water coolers and monitors at the lowest prices across Jharkhand. We are an authorized reseller of brands like Lapcare, Sony, Samsung, and Voltas.


We include our capabilities in industrial automation, particularly in the steel plant sector, delivering advanced systems for process optimization and equipment management. We also provide staffing and business process outsourcing (BPO) services, enabling our clients to focus on core operations while SSI handles workforce and process efficiencies.
