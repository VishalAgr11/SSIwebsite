About Us


Founded in 1994 and headquartered in Jamshedpur, India, SSI has built a legacy of delivering tailored IT solutions and consumer products to a diverse clientele. Over the years, the company has mastered the art of integrating modern technology into traditional business practices, enabling clients to thrive in competitive markets. With its roots in the steel industry, SSI has successfully diversified into retail electronics, staffing, and IT consulting, solidifying its position as a dynamic service provider.

The company’s mission is to combine state-of-the-art technology with innovative methodologies, providing clients with cost-effective and high-performance solutions. SSI takes pride in its client-centric approach, ensuring that every project meets the customer’s needs. The company is driven by values such as integrity, accountability, and innovation, fostering long-term relationships with clients and partners.

Through a team of highly skilled professionals, SSI delivers end-to-end IT services, including project management, system integration, and infrastructure setup. Its focus on continuous improvement and embracing cutting-edge technology has enabled SSI to remain a trusted partner for businesses across multiple sectors.
